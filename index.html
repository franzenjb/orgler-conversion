How to Use This Tool

Step-by-step guide for creating embeddable Power BI links

1

Open Your Power BI Report

From the Group workspace in Power BI Service, open the report from the Reports tab (not Dashboards) within the workspace.

2

Navigate to Desired Page

Navigate to the report page you want the report to default to when consumed by your RC View user.

3

Copy the Power BI URL

From the browser address bar, copy the entire URL. It should look like this:

https://app.powerbi.com/groups/[group-id]/reports/[report-id]/[section]
4

Paste and Convert

Paste your Power BI URL into the converter above and click "Convert to Orgler URL".

5

Use in Story Map/RCView

Copy the generated Orgler URL and use it as your embed link in RC View or ArcGIS Story Maps.
